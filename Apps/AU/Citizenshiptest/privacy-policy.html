Effective date: 29 June 2026

In short: we collect nothing. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no ads and no tracking. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal information or usage data. There is no sign-up, no account, and no analytics or advertising SDK of any kind.

How your study data is handled

Your study progress — answers, mistakes, statistics and settings — is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to us or to anyone else. The app works fully offline. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Purchases

Pro subscriptions are offered as in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Payment and billing are handled by Apple under its own privacy policy; we do not receive your name, payment details or any other personal information. Purchase entitlements are verified on your device.

Third-party services

The app uses no third-party services. The only external party involved is Apple, for processing in-app purchases as described above.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data from anyone, it also collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this policy and its effective date before the change takes effect.
